A BROKEN WING

A broken wing,
No means to fly,
But aspirations still,
Oh so high,
I wouldn't lie,
Pretty sure you cry,
Never meant to break,
A love so high

The pearls which roll,
Down your eyes,
I sure love them all,
But can't resist their fall
Different language they speak,
All out of despair
The only reason I,
But now can't make a pair.

Now we live,
Five feet apart,
But sounds like,
A dozen miles apart
We cover the distance
For an Instance
But fall back,
Into our rack.
